# Step 04: Update Jira

**Purpose:** Sync the sprint allocation with Jira backlog.

## Instructions

1. Use Jira MCP to transition allocated stories to "Selected for Development"
2. Assign stories to the Developer role
3. Move stories from "Backlog" to the appropriate sprint column

## Output

- `jira_update_results`: Summary of Jira updates
- `sprint_board_url`: Link to the updated sprint board
