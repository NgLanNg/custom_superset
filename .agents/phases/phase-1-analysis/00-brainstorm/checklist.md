# Phase 1: Brainstorming

## Execution Checklist

### Step 01: Connect & Align

- [ ] Review current context (`vault/active_context.md`).
- [ ] Understand the core problem by asking clarifying questions.
- [ ] Define Purpose, Constraints, and Success Criteria.

### Step 02: Explore & Ideate

- [ ] Internally apply SCAMPER, Reverse Brainstorming, Starbursting, and 5 Whys.
- [ ] Synthesize internal exploration into 2-3 distinct architectural or feature approaches.
- [ ] Present generated approaches (with Pros/Cons, Effort, and Risks) to the user.
- [ ] Iterate and refine the chosen option based on feedback.

### Step 03: Synthesize & Design

- [ ] Score approaches using: (Impact × Feasibility) / Risk (if applicable).
- [ ] Present a clear recommendation or direction to the user.
- [ ] Draft the initial design in 200-300 word chunks and validate each section.
- [ ] Save the finalized design to `docs/plans/YYYY-MM-DD-{topic}-design.md`.

- [ ] **NYQUIST GATE**: Verify the design addresses the core goals without prematurely over-engineering.
