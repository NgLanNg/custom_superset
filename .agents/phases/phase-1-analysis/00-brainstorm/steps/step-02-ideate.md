# Step 02: Explore & Ideate

## STEP GOAL

Focuses on structured generation of approaches by providing a menu of brainstorming methods and executing the chosen method collaboratively with the user.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES (READ FIRST)

- 🛑 NEVER generate content without user input.
- 📖 CRITICAL: Read the complete step file before taking any action.
- 📋 YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator.
- ✅ YOU MUST ALWAYS SPEAK OUTPUT In your Agent communication style.

## EXECUTION PROTOCOLS

- 🎯 One question at a time. Multiple choice preferred.
- 🎯 YAGNI ruthlessly.
- 🎯 Focus strictly on exploring options and expanding the design space.

## CONTEXT BOUNDARIES

- Available context: Current workspace, `./vault/active_context.md`, and the problem statement defined in Step 01.
- Focus: Idea generation, exploring constraints, identifying edge cases.
- Limits: Avoid prematurely locking into a single architectural choice.

## 📋 Steps

### A) Internal Generation

1. **Before speaking to the user**, use the following frameworks internally to explore the problem space:
   - `SCAMPER`: How can we Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other uses, Eliminate, or Reverse elements of the problem?
   - `Reverse Brainstorming`: What are the failure points? How could this go wrong?
   - `Starbursting`: Who, What, When, Where, Why, How?
   - `5 Whys`: What is the root cause we are really solving?
2. Synthesize your internal brainstorming into 2-3 distinct, viable architectural or feature approaches.
3. For each approach, define:
   - Description
   - Pros & Cons
   - Estimated Effort
   - Key risks identified during reverse brainstorming

### B) Collaborative Exploration

1. Present the 2-3 generated approaches to the user.
2. Ask the user one clarifying question about their preference or concerns regarding the presented options.
3. Wait for the user's input.
4. Iterate and refine the chosen option based on feedback.
