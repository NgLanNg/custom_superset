# Phase 4: Quick Flow

## Execution Checklist

### Step 01: Triage & Routine Check

- [ ] Confirm the task is a "Quick Flow" candidate (low risk, small scope).
- [ ] Choose the appropriate sub-flow: `quick-dev`, `quick-dev-new-preview`, or `quick-spec`.

### Step 02: Implementation & Self-Check

- [ ] Follow the micro-steps for the chosen sub-flow.
- [ ] Perform a rigorous self-check against the original requirement.
- [ ] Conduct an internal adversarial review to find edge cases.

### Step 03: Finalization & Review

- [ ] Resolve all findings from the self-check and review.
- [ ] Present the finalized change or specification to the user.

- [ ] **NYQUIST GATE**: Verify that the "Quick Flow" didn't bypass essential testing or documentation.
