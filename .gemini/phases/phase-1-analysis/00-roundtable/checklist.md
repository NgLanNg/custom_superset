# Phase 1: Boss Roundtable

## Execution Checklist

### Step 01: Assemble The Squad

- [ ] Identify the expert personas required for the debate (e.g., Architect, Security, Pragmatist).
- [ ] Define the core problem or decision to be debated.

### Step 02: Collaborative Debate

- [ ] Facilitate a multi-perspective discussion using subagents.
- [ ] Ensure all personas provide critical feedback and identify trade-offs.
- [ ] Push for consensus on the most balanced solution.

### Step 03: Synthesis & Documentation

- [ ] Summarize the strategic consensus and final recommendation.
- [ ] Generate the Decision Record in `docs/decisions/YYYY-MM-DD-{topic}.md`.

- [ ] **NYQUIST GATE**: Verify that a diversity of perspectives was actually captured and synthesized into the final decision.
